The National Commission for the Control of Personal Data Protection ,
met on December 18, 2015, under the chairmanship of Mr. Saïd Ihraï;
Considering the Law n ° 09-08 promulgated by the Dahir 1-09-15, of February 18, 2009, relating to the protection
of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data (BO n °
5714 of 05/03/2009);
Considering the Decree n ° 2-09-165 of May 21, 2009 taken for the application of the aforementioned Law n ° 09-08
(BO n ° 5744 of 06/18/2009);

1

Having regard to the Internal Regulations of the CNDP (approved by decision of the Prime Minister n ° 3-33-11
of March 28, 2011 / BO n ° 5932 of 04/07/2011);
And in accordance with the provisions of article 43 of Law 09-08 which attribute to the
Commission the power to establish the list of States recognized as ensuring a level of
sufficient protection of the privacy and fundamental rights and freedoms of persons
with regard to the processing of personal data, has decided as follows:

Article 1
Subject to notifying the CNDP, in accordance with the appropriate regime, of any transfer of data to
personal character abroad, and following the examination of national legislation and
respective internal procedures for the protection of personal data, the
Commission considers that the following countries offer a sufficient level of protection
and complies with the requirements of Moroccan legislation relating to the protection of persons
physical with regard to the processing of personal data:
