REGULATION
From the Personal Data Protection Authority:
PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION INSTITUTION
DISCIPLINE SUPERVISOR REGULATION
Goal
ARTICLE 1 – (1) The purpose of this Regulation is to assign the personnel of the Personal Data Protection Authority to the disciplinary superiors and
to determine the procedures and principles regarding the disciplinary practice.
Scope
ARTICLE 2 - (1) This Regulation was issued by the Personal Data Protection Authority, dated 14/7/1965 and numbered 657.
It covers the personnel who work subject to the Civil Servants Law.
Rest
ARTICLE 3 - (1) This Regulation is governed by Article 124 of the Civil Servants Law No. 657 and 17/9/1982
About Disciplinary Boards and Disciplinary Chiefs, which was put into effect by the Council of Ministers Decision dated and numbered 8/5336
It has been prepared based on the provisions of Article 16 of the Regulation.
Disciplinary supervisors
ARTICLE 4 – (1) Disciplinary and senior disciplinary supervisors of the Personal Data Protection Authority personnel
It is shown in the ruler.
Disciplinary procedures and principles
ARTICLE 5 – (1) In terms of the procedures and principles regarding discipline, the Law on Civil Servants No. 657 and Discipline
The provisions of the Regulation on Boards and Disciplinary Supervisors are applied.
Force
ARTICLE 6 – (1) This Regulation enters into force on the date of publication.
Executive
ARTICLE 7 – (1) The provisions of this Regulation are executed by the President of the Personal Data Protection Authority.
