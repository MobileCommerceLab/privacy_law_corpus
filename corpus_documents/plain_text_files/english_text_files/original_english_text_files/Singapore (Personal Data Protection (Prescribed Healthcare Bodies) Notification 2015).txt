PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION ACT 2012
(ACT 26 OF 2012)
PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION
(PRESCRIBED HEALTHCARE BODIES)
NOTIFICATION 2015
ARRANGEMENT OF PARAGRAPHS
Paragraph
1.
Citation and commencement
2.
Prescribed healthcare bodies
The Schedule

In exercise of the powers conferred by the definition of “prescribed
healthcare body” in section 2(1) of the Personal Data Protection Act
2012, the Minister for Health hereby makes the following
Notification:
Citation and commencement
1. This Notification may be cited as the Personal Data Protection
(Prescribed Healthcare Bodies) Notification 2015 and comes into
operation on 1 March 2015.
Prescribed healthcare bodies
2. The organisations specified in the Schedule are prescribed as
prescribed healthcare bodies for the purposes of the Second Schedule
to the Act.
